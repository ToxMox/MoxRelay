NOTES
-----

OBS plugins (7)
    The bundled OBS plugins shipped under obs-plugins/ are image-source,
    obs-ffmpeg, obs-filters, obs-text, win-capture, win-dshow and win-wasapi.
    They are part of OBS Studio and share its GPLv2-or-later license and
    corresponding source.

FFmpeg / x264
    These are GPL-enabled builds. They make the binary as a whole GPL. See
    SOURCES.txt for the pinned versions and corresponding source.

Qt 6 (LGPLv3, dynamically linked)
    Qt is used as unmodified, dynamically-linked shared libraries (Qt6Core,
    Qt6Gui, Qt6Widgets and the qwindows platform plugin). These DLLs ship side
    by side with the executable and may be replaced by the user with an
    interface-compatible build of Qt 6, which satisfies the LGPLv3 relinking
    right (LGPLv3 section 4). LGPLv3 incorporates GPLv3 by reference; the GPLv3
    text is the root LICENSE file. See licenses/Qt6/LICENSE.LGPL3.

libsrt (MPL-2.0)
    Upstream  : https://github.com/Haivision/srt
    Copyright (c) 2018 Haivision Systems Inc.

    A standalone Secure Reliable Transport (SRT) streaming library supplied
    by the obs-deps bundle and shipped as srt.dll next to the executable.
    Licensed under the Mozilla Public License, Version 2.0. The full MPL-2.0
    text ships at licenses/libsrt/LICENSE; a copy can also be obtained at
    https://mozilla.org/MPL/2.0/.

libdatachannel
    Licensed under MPL-2.0 (licenses/libdatachannel/LICENSE). The prebuilt
    libdatachannel binary statically bundles its own dependencies (libjuice,
    libsrtp, usrsctp, plog, nlohmann/json); their verified per-dependency
    notices are reproduced in the LIBDATACHANNEL BUNDLED DEPENDENCIES section
    below.

MSVC C++ runtime
    The Microsoft Visual C++ runtime DLLs (vcruntime140, vcruntime140_1,
